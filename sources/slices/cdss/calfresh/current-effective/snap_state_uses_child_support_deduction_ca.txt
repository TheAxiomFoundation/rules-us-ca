California Department of Social Services, California DSS Manual FS, Manual
Letter No. FS-06-04, section 63-502.2(p):

Child support payments that a household member pays to or for an individual
living outside of the household.

The child support exclusion is the monthly amount of child support payments that
a household member, with a legal obligation to pay child support payments to or
for an individual living outside of the household, actually makes.

Payments are excluded only to the extent that they represent the household's
child support obligation which has been ordered by a court or administrative
authority.
